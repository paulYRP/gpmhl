Our research is focused on three main areas: (1) understanding how genes and sport interact to influence human health and performance, (2) understanding what challenges and opportunities we can discover by stuying neurogenomics, (3) understanding how we can use generative modelling to design solutions to address grand challenges in health, biology, and sports, and (4) understanding how immunogenomics interacts with mental health and performance.
